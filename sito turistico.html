Axum, also spelled Aksum is a town in the Tigray Region of Ethiopia with a population of 66,900 residents (as of 2015).[2] It is the site of the historic capital of the Aksumite Empire.[3] Axum is located in the Central Zone of the Tigray Region, near the base of the Adwa mountains.
